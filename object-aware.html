Object Awareness

Object awareness is the ability of CynLr robots to identify, understand, and interact with objects of varying shapes, sizes, and materials in any orientation.

This capability allows our robots to work with objects they've never seen before, adapting their gripping strategy in real-time based on the physical properties they detect.

Traditional robots require precise positioning and specialized end-effectors for each object type. CynLr robots overcome this limitation with advanced contextual understanding and adaptive manipulation.
